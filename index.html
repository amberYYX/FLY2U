Background

FLY2U project is for developing a platform for fast and cheap cross-regional transportation using available space of passengers’ luggage in the Digital Nomad era. There are always some messages asking to get small things from other countries in different chatting groups. Why are traditional courier companies not their first choice? Our question starts from this problem recognition. That is why we bring this innovative application on the market in this time!

Our Project

Making use of travelers’ available luggage space! That is our core idea. FLY2U is premium low-cost transportation service using reliable airline. People who want to share their empty baggage space, or who want to transit/receive what they want from/to abroad can use this platform! We found our value in terms of 'The best value, safe and available'. Actually, in social networking app, The relationship between supply and demand is not clear. Traditional courier companies can be too slow for cheap delivery plan, and too expensive for fast delivery plan. We are connecting people who need the service and service provider with simple and efficient process. All the steps will be regulated by the system. We truly believe that we have strong business model with solid revenue stream.

Business Document PRESENTATION SLIDES

Featured Functions

  • One-click posting and registration

    • Do not need useless information to make a deal. We take a responsibility to exchange money and service!

  • Intuitive service information display

    • Simple progress checking and real-time chatting with service provider to track the order safely

  • Secure payment system

    • The service fee will be frozen until users receive their package

  • Transportation insurance

    • In case of damage during parcel transportation, users can include insurance to their contract.

  • Self pick-up cabinet
    • Place self-pick-up cabinets at the airport and regional center to facilitate the delivery of parcels

For Buyers

  • Search function to find out best offer for them
  • Chatting service to bargain the price
  • Automatic fee calculation based on weights
  • Tracking service based on flight itenerary
  • Payment safety methods: insurance and freezing

For Service Providers (Travelers)

  • Four levels of order for efficient management: Posted, Bargaining, Delivering, and Delivered
  • Accept/Decline function based on buyers' request
  • Chatting service to bargain the price
  • Easy posting: only need flight number at the first time.
  • Easy validation using product photo and flight number
